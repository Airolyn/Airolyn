Privacy Policy
Your privacy is our priority. Learn exactly how Airolyn protects your personal information and keeps your air quality data secure.
Airolyn does not collect unnecessary data and never sells or shares your personal information. The app is designed to keep all sensitive details secure with industry-standard encryption and zero third-party tracking. We use only location and basic authentication details for your own app experience (and only if you choose), transparently explained in this policy. No ads, no data selling, no nonsense—just trusted air quality alerts. If you have any privacy concern, we're always happy to help.

What Data We Collect

  • Location Data: Used only for showing accurate local air quality and AQI. Not stored or shared.
  • Authentication: If you sign in, only basic data for login is securely stored with Firebase.
  • No Advertisements: We do not show ads or track you for commercial gain.

How We Use It

  • Display real-time AQI and air pollution info for your region.
  • Send you health-based notifications and air quality alerts.
  • App analytics (crash logs, performance) to improve app reliability. No personal identity is logged here.

Your Choices

  • You can turn off location or notifications any time in your device or app settings.
  • You may request your Firebase account be deleted anytime via the app.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact our team in-app or at airolyn.page@gmail.com.
